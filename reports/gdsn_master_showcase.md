## Executive Overview

GDSN-X™ is a **multi-domain decision intelligence system** designed to evaluate strategic risk across complex, real-world environments.

The system applies a **structured, deterministic risk model** integrating:

- Economic Risk  
- Political Risk  
- Social Risk  

to generate **quantified, interpretable, and actionable decision insights**.

GDSN-X™ enables organizations to:

- Convert uncertainty into structured analysis  
- Identify dominant risk drivers  
- Improve strategic decision quality  
- Apply consistent evaluation across domains  

The system bridges the gap between:

> **Qualitative judgment ↔ Quantitative intelligence**

---

## Risk Model Framework

Risk Score is calculated using:

R = (0.40 × E) + (0.35 × P) + (0.25 × S)

Where:

- E = Economic Risk (0–100)  
- P = Political Risk (0–100)  
- S = Social Risk (0–100)  

---

## Risk Classification

| Score Range | Risk Level |
|------------|-----------|
| 0 – 39     | Low Risk |
| 40 – 69    | Medium Risk |
| 70 – 100   | High Risk |
